Exercising everyday and eating the right foods helps keep him away and ensure your mind is super sharp.

Cruddy Cancer

Crudy Cancer is a slient sneaky vilain. He creeps in undetected and mutates the cells of your body. Over time, these cancer cells grow and take over your good cells making you feel weak. Crudy Cancer does not discriminate and tries to go after many cells in your body like bone, blood, liver, lung, and brain.

Dangerous Diabetes

When Dangerous Diabetes lurks around, this means you have too much sugar in you body. Sugar exists in most foods we eat and our body uses it for energy to move and stay active. Insuline made by our body, works like a lock to a door. When the door is open and unlocked, sugar can travel from our bloodstream into our cells giving us energy. Dangerous Diabetes likes to hide this key and keep the door locked leading to too much sugar in our bloodstream. Sugar that never makes it into our cells. Eating the right foods and staying active are a great way to keep Diabetes away from the key.

Awful Asthma

Awful Asthma is just the worst! He goes after the airways in our lungs and makes it hard to breathe. When he attacks, you may feel tightness in your chest, cough, or wheeze. May things around us invite Astham into our body like second hand cigarette smoke, pet dander, dust mites, and air polution.

Overactive Oscar

Overactive Oscar just won't sit still and keeps stopping you from paying attention to what's important. When Oscar is around suddenly you hear, see, and notice everything around you. So much is happening that your body can't figure out who or what to listen to. He also has a knack for getting you to squirm in your seat. What a little trouble maker he is.

King Constipation

King Constipation is a tough guy to get rid of. He likes to hang out in your colon and makes your poop hard and painful to get out. He usually comes to visit when your don't drink enough liquids or eat foods with enough fiber (like the Rainbow Superheroes). Exercising regularly is also a great way to keep him away.
